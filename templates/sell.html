Create Account

Creating your MyCart seller account is a quick and hassle-free process that takes less than 10 minutes. You only need basic details like your name, phone number, email ID, and GST information to get started. Once registered, you gain access to the MyCart Seller Dashboard where you can manage your store easily. MyCart ensures a smooth onboarding experience so even first-time sellers can join without confusion. The platform guides you step-by-step in setting up your profile, verifying your documents, and activating your seller account. After verification, you can instantly start listing products and selling to customers. MyCart also provides support during the entire setup process to help you begin your journey confidently.

  • Quick 10-minute registration process
  • Simple KYC and GST document upload
  • Easy dashboard to manage your store
  • Guided onboarding steps
  • Instant access after verification

List Products

Listing products on MyCart is simple, even for new sellers. The platform provides user-friendly tools for uploading product photos, descriptions, prices, and variations. You can list items individually or upload them in bulk using templates. MyCart guides you on how to create high-quality listings that attract buyers. Once your products are added, they become visible to millions of customers searching across categories. You can also update stock, edit details, and track performance anytime. Clear listing guidelines ensure that your catalog looks professional and competitive in the marketplace

  • Easy product upload tools

Storage & Shipping

MyCart offers reliable storage and shipping solutions that make order delivery effortless. Sellers can store products in their own warehouse or choose MyCart Fulfillment Centers for faster dispatch. MyCart’s delivery partners handle packaging, pickup, and doorstep delivery to customers across India. Real-time tracking allows both sellers and buyers to monitor shipments easily. The end-to-end logistics support ensures that orders are delivered safely and on time. This reduces the workload on sellers and improves the overall customer experience. With reliable storage and quick shipping, your products reach customers faster, boosting ratings and repeat sales.

  • Store products yourself or use MyCart warehouses
  • Fast shipping across India
  • Real-time order tracking
  • Professional packaging & delivery
  • Better seller ratings through quick dispatch

Receive Payments

MyCart ensures that sellers receive payments safely, quickly, and without complications. Once an order is delivered, the payment is securely processed and added to your seller wallet. Every 7 days, the amount is transferred directly to your registered bank account. The payment dashboard allows you to track your earnings, view settlements, check deductions, and download reports. This transparency helps you manage your business finances effectively. MyCart also protects sellers from fraudulent transactions, ensuring that you are paid for every valid order.

  • Secure payments every 7 days
  • Direct transfer to bank account
  • Transparent settlement reports
  • Fraud protection for sellers
  • Easy-to-track earnings
